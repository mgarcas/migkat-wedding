Arriving to Providence

You can reach Providence by flying directly to Providence or Boston. See below for some considerations.

Providence - There are no direct flights between Miami International Airport and Providence T.F. Green Airport. You will need to connect, most conenctions are through DC or Charlotte and are rather short and smooth. If you fly from Ft Lauderdale Airport, Jet Blue has a non stop flight to T.F. Green. Having said that, the airport in Providence is very nice, not crowded at all, and 15 to 20 minutes to Dowtown Providence and the hotels.
Boston - There are direct flights from Miami International and Ft. Lauderdale airports to Boston Logan Airport. Boston Logan is about 52 miles from Providence. If driving, it could take between 1.5-2 hours, depending on traffic. There are trains and buses that can take you from Boston to Providence, please reach out to us if you're considering those routes, as we have lots of experience riding those railways.
