About Us

Katherine and Miguel met in Madrid when Katherine was on vacation with a dear friend. Being in a long distance relationship, they stayed in contact through texts, emails, and videocalls.

Sharing poems and songs was how we communicated our feelings across the distance. The poem below is exceptionally special to us as it speaks about the “dreaming” to be together when you're apart. We feel so lucky that you will share this special weekend with us as we close the distance happily ever after.

Pathways

Rainer Maria Rilke

Understand, I'll slip quietly
away from the noisy crowd
when I see the pale
stars rising, blooming, over the oaks.

I'll pursue solitary pathways
through the pale twilit meadows,
with only this one dream:

You come too.
